# Welcome to Our Documentation Site!

## Introduction

Welcome to our documentation site, the central hub for comprehensive information about our projects, processes, and tools. Whether you are a developer, marketer, or providing support to our students, you'll find valuable resources here to get started and stay informed. Look around and fill in any gaps!

## Getting Started

### 1. Explore Our Tools

Check out the various tools we offer:

- [User Dashboard](https://prd-xms-api.combinedcuriosity.io/admin/users/)
- [LMS Admin](https://prd-xms-api.combinedcuriosity.io/lms/resources/)
- [Shipments Dashboard](https://prd-xms-api.combinedcuriosity.io/admin/shipping/)
- [Brand Dashboard](https://prd-xms-api.combinedcuriosity.io/admin/brands/)

_**Note:** You'll need to log in to access these tools. Use your LMS credentials to log in. If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it through the "forgot password" link in the LMS. For account creation, please post in [#dev-help](https://combinedcuriosity.slack.com/archives/C042JJE86AH)._

### 2. Browse Documentation

Explore the documentation by navigating through the sidebar on the left or use the search bar at the top to find information quickly. Our documentation is thoughtfully organized to provide a clear and intuitive understanding of our projects.

## Updates and Notifications

Stay in the loop with the latest changes, updates, and announcements:

- Watch the [product releases](https://combinedcuriosity.slack.com/archives/C043FAB9196) channel for timely updates on new features and improvements.

## Feedback

Your feedback is incredibly valuable to us! Whether you have an idea for a new feature or encounter any issues, we encourage you to join the conversation. The best place for feedback is in our Slack workspace.

You can click the Slack icon above, the `Question? Give us feedback` link to the right, or click this link ([#dev-help](https://combinedcuriosity.slack.com/archives/C042JJE86AH)) to be taken to our channel.
