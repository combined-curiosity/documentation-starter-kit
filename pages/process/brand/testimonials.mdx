# Testimonials

These are the testimonials that appear on the brand's payment modal.

## How to add a testimonial

<Steps>
  ### Navigate to the [Brand Dashboard](https://prd-xms-api.combinedcuriosity.io/admin/brands/).
  ### Click the "Testimonials" tab.
  ### Click the plus icon next to the section title.
  ### Fill in any necessary information in the form.
  ### Click the "Create" button.
  ### You will receive a confirmation message about the successful testimonial creation.
  ### The page will refresh, and you will see the new testimonial entry.
</Steps>

## How to edit an existing testimonial

<Steps>
  ### Navigate to the [Brand Dashboard](https://prd-xms-api.combinedcuriosity.io/admin/brands/).
  ### Click the pencil and paper icon next to the entry you would like to edit.
  ### Make any necessary changes in the form.
  ### Click the "Update" button when finished.
  ### A confirmation message will indicate the successful update of the testimonial.
  ### The page will refresh, and you will see the updated testimonial entry.
</Steps>

## How to archive an existing testimonial

<Steps>
  ### Navigate to the [Brand Dashboard](https://prd-xms-api.combinedcuriosity.io/admin/brands/).
  ### Click the file box icon next to the entry you would like to archive.
  ### A confirmation box will appear. Select OK to proceed.
  ### Look for the confirmation message indicating the successful archiving of the testimonial.
  ### The section will refresh, and the testimonial will no longer be displayed.
</Steps>
